Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our real estate services

What areas in Lagos do you cover?

We cover all major areas in Lagos including Maitama, Asokoro, Gwarimpa, Wuse, Central Business District, Jabi, Utako, and surrounding neighborhoods. Our services extend to both residential and commercial properties across the Federal Capital Territory.

How do you verify property documents?

We have a dedicated legal team that verifies all property documents including Certificate of Occupancy, Governor's Consent, Survey Plans, and other relevant documents. We work with relevant government agencies to ensure all properties we list have genuine and verifiable documentation.

What are your fees for property sales?

Our commission rates are competitive and transparent. For property sales, we typically charge a percentage of the final sale price, which varies based on the property value and services required. We provide a detailed breakdown of all fees during our initial consultation with no hidden charges.

Do you offer property management for overseas owners?

Yes, we specialize in managing properties for overseas owners. We provide regular updates, photos, financial reports, and handle all aspects of property management including tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance, and emergency repairs. We make it easy for you to own property in Nigeria while living abroad.

How long does the property buying process take?

The timeline varies depending on the property type, documentation status, and financing method. Typically, cash purchases can be completed within 4-6 weeks, while mortgage transactions may take 8-12 weeks. We work efficiently to expedite the process while ensuring all legal requirements are properly met.
