Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

What are your office hours?

Our office is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays, though emergency contact numbers are available for urgent matters.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-4 business hours during office hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our hotline at +234 902 405 6004 for immediate assistance.

Do you offer virtual property viewings?

Yes, we offer comprehensive virtual viewing options including video tours, live video calls with our agents, and detailed photo galleries. This is especially convenient for international clients or those unable to visit properties in person.

What areas in Abuja do you serve?

We serve all major areas in Abuja including Maitama, Asokoro, Gwarimpa, Wuse, Central Business District, Jabi, Utako, Katampe, and surrounding neighborhoods. Our services cover both residential and commercial properties across the Federal Capital Territory.

Can I schedule property viewings on weekends?

Yes, we accommodate property viewings on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Sunday viewings can be arranged by special appointment for serious buyers or tenants. We recommend scheduling weekend viewings at least 24 hours in advance.
