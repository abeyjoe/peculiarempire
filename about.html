Our Journey

From humble beginnings to Lagos's leading real estate company

2012

Foundation

Peculiar Empire Int Ltd was founded with a vision to revolutionize real estate services in Lagos. Started with a small team of 3 dedicated professionals.

2014

First Major Success

Completed our first major commercial property transaction worth ₦250 million. Expanded our team to 10 professionals and opened our first branch in Maitama.

2016

Market Leadership

Became one of Lagos's top 5 real estate companies. Launched our property management division and expanded services to include luxury properties.

2018

Technology Integration

Launched our digital platform with virtual tours and online property valuation tools. Expanded our team to 25 professionals across 3 branches in Lagos.

2020

Industry Recognition

Received the "Best Real Estate Company in Lagos" award. Surpassed 1,000 successful property transactions with over ₦8 billion in total property value.

2025

Present Day

Now serving over 2,500 clients with a team of 35 professionals. Continuing to innovate and set new standards in Nigeria's real estate industry.
